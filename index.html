Josh Shapiro

Josh Shapiro is a Head of Research at Tonal, a company that builds the world's most intelligent home gym.

He leads research and development of Tonal's machine learning systems including multimodal LLMs, generative personalized programming, predictive strength modeling, and real-time weight calibration algorithms.

Previously, Josh worked at Kensho as a Machine Learning Engineer developing multimodal document understanding models.
